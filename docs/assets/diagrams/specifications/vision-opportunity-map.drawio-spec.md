# draw.io Design Specification — Vision Opportunity Map

- File name: vision-opportunity-map.drawio
- Source context: docs/01-Vision/vision.md

## Purpose
Create a presentation-ready strategic map showing how market forces create school pain, which EduSync capabilities address that pain, and which business outcomes result.

## Components
- Market forces
  - Mobile parent expectations
  - Digital payments adoption
  - Board and compliance complexity
  - AI adoption
  - School chain growth
- School pain points
  - Communication fragmentation
  - Manual fee collection
  - Reporting burden
  - Administrative overload
  - Branch visibility gap
- EduSync capabilities
  - Unified notifications
  - Integrated billing and payment
  - Structured reports
  - AI-assisted workflows
  - Multi-branch dashboards
- Business outcomes
  - Higher parent trust
  - Improved cash flow
  - Management readiness
  - Productivity gains
  - Scalable governance

## Layout
- Use a wide landscape page.
- Organize the canvas into four horizontal bands:
  1. Market forces
  2. School pain
  3. EduSync capability
  4. Business outcome
- Place each band in a subtle container to preserve alignment.
- Keep arrow paths short and evenly spaced to avoid visual clutter.

## Connections
- Connect each market force to one related pain point.
- Connect each pain point to one capability.
- Connect each capability to one outcome.
- Use directional arrows with consistent spacing and avoid crossing lines where possible.

## Color Recommendations
- Market forces: teal (#2E86AB)
- School pain: amber (#D97706)
- EduSync capability: blue (#2563EB)
- Business outcome: green (#15803D)
- Background: light gray (#F8FAFC)

## Swimlanes
- Recommended swimlanes: Market, Pain, Capability, Outcome
- Use thin divider lines between lanes to keep the diagram readable.
